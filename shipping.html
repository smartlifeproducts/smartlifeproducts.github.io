Shipping and Delivery

Thank you for shopping with Smart Life Products. This page provides information on our shipping and delivery policies. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to contact us.

1. Shipping Information

1.1 Shipping Locations

We currently ship within india only. If your location is not listed, please contact us to inquire about shipping options.

1.2 Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:
Standard Shipping: Delivery within 3 business days. Shipping times may vary depending on your location and the availability of items.

1.3 Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your shipping location. You can view the shipping cost during the checkout process. We offer free shipping on orders over 5000 (if applicable).

2. Order Processing


2.1 Order Processing Time


Orders are processed within 1 business days of receipt. Orders placed after 5 PM will be processed the next business day. Processing time does not include shipping time.

2.2 Order Confirmation


Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with your order details and a tracking number (if applicable).

4. Delivery Issues


4.1 Delays


While we strive to ensure timely delivery, delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather, carrier issues, or high order volume. We appreciate your patience and understanding.

4.2 Lost or Damaged Shipments


If your shipment is lost or damaged, please contact us within 2 days of the expected delivery date. We will assist you in resolving the issue and provide a replacement or refund as appropriate.
